# Test Questions for HK Legal Chatbot (Cap. 282)

This document contains a set of test questions to evaluate the accuracy and performance of the HK Legal Chatbot, specifically focusing on **Cap. 282 Employees' Compensation Ordinance**.

## 1. Factual Questions (Direct Retrieval)
- What is the maximum amount of funeral expenses payable under Cap. 282?
- How is "earnings" defined under the Employees' Compensation Ordinance?
- What is the time limit for a workman to give notice of an accident to his employer?
- Does the Ordinance apply to domestic helpers?
- What are the compulsory insurance requirements for employers under Section 40?

## 2. Scenario-Based Questions (Reasoning)
- If an employee is injured while traveling to work during a Typhoon Signal No. 8, is the employer liable for compensation?
- An employee suffered a permanent partial incapacity of 15%. How is the lump sum compensation calculated if they are 40 years old?
- If an employer fails to pay the periodic payments for temporary incapacity, what are the penalties?
- A construction worker was injured while working for a sub-contractor. Can he claim compensation directly from the main contractor?
- What happens if an employee's injury was caused by their own "serious and wilful misconduct"?

## 3. Edge Cases & Complex Queries
- Are "occupational diseases" covered the same way as "accidents"? (Reference Section 32)
- What is the "Minimum Insurance Cover" required for a company with 150 employees?
- If an accident happens outside of Hong Kong, is the employee still covered?
- How does the Ordinance handle compensation for "Medical Expenses" incurred in mainland China?
- What is the role of the "Employees' Compensation Assistance Fund Board"?

## 4. Comparison & Multi-Section Queries
- What is the difference between "Permanent Total Incapacity" and "Permanent Partial Incapacity" in terms of compensation calculation?
- List the schedules that define the "Specified Amount of Compensation".
- How do the notice requirements in Section 11 differ from the claim requirements in Section 14?

---
**Note:** When testing, verify if the chatbot provides the correct **Section** or **Schedule** citation as a reference.
